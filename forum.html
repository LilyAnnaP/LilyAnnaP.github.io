Krasno Global Policy Forum

Inaugural Krasno Global Policy Forum (Nov. 17-18, 2025)

Organized by Prof. Klaus Larres, UNC-Chapel Hill & Christian Forstner, Hanns-Seidel Foundation

Hosted in the Pleasants Family Room, Wilson Library, UNC-Chapel Hill.

For parking information, please see visitor parking options here.
