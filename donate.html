Donations & Contributions

THE KRASNO EVENTS SERIES is a not-for-profit series of lectures, talks and discussions to enhance our understanding of and insights into global affairs in both past and present. It is financed by contributions and donations kindly provided by a number of centers and institutions within UNC-Chapel Hill and some generous members of the general public.

Tax-deductible donations and contributions by members of the general public are greatly appreciated. This helps us significantly to maintain and expand the program.

If you feel you would like to contribute to support the Krasno Events series by means for a tax deductible donation, then please make out a check to the "Arts and Sciences Foundation." Please write into the memo section of the check "Krasno events series - Designation #104492."

Send check to:Jennifer Parker, Business Manager, Dept. of History, Hamilton Hall #3195, UNC-Chapel Hill, Chapel Hill, NC, 27599-3195.

Or Send Check to: Prof. Klaus Larres, Dept. of History, Hamilton Hall #3195, UNC-Chapel Hill, Chapel Hill, NC, 27599-3195.

You will receive a receipt for your tax deduction from the UNC Arts and Sciences Foundation in due course.
